Our story

Crafting Dallas's Finest Woodwork Since 2008

To deliver exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service, creating woodwork that enhances lives and stands the test of time.

Best Carpentry was founded in 2008 by master carpenter John Davis, who began his career learning traditional woodworking techniques from his grandfather. After years of honing his skills on residential and commercial projects, John established Best Carpentry with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted team of skilled artisans serving the Dallas metro area.

Over the years, we have completed hundreds of projects ranging from custom furniture to full home renovations. Our reputation is built on meticulous attention to detail, honest communication, and a passion for woodworking. We believe that every piece we create should be functional, beautiful, and built to last for generations.
